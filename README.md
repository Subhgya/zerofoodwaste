ZeroFoodWaste

Feed People, Not Landfills

ZeroFoodWaste is a tech-driven platform designed to reduce food waste by connecting surplus food providers with people in need. It enables restaurants, households, and event organizers to donate excess food efficiently while ensuring timely distribution through NGOs and volunteers.

🚀 Product Vision

To create a world where no edible food is wasted by seamlessly connecting surplus food sources with those in need through smart and scalable technology.

🔭 Features
📍 Real-Time Food Listing – Add surplus food with location & quantity
🔔 Instant Notifications – Alert nearby NGOs & volunteers
🗺️ Live Map Integration – Discover food availability around you
⏰ Expiry-Based Priority – Urgent pickups for perishable food
🚚 Pickup & Delivery Coordination
⭐ Trust & Rating System
📊 Analytics Dashboard – Track food saved & impact
🧑‍🤝‍🧑 Users
Food Providers – Restaurants, hotels, households
Receivers – NGOs, shelters, orphanages
Volunteers – Delivery & coordination support
